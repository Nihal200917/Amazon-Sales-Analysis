Amazon Sales Profit Prediction

Introduction

Sales Profit by preferred Sales Channels have grown tremendously. I gathered information from Amazon Sales Kaggle dataset, covering 100 occurred sales throughout years from 2010 to 2019. we used this information to predict the result of sales profits in upcoming years depending on sales channels. We modeled Total Profit, a numerical variable, as a linear function of Order Years. As year increases Profit seams to increase as the year advances.

Exploratory Data Analysis

Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) is a crucial step in the data analysis process where we systematically examine and summarize the data to uncover patterns, identify anomalies, test hypotheses, and check assumptions. Through EDA, we gain a deeper understanding of the data, which helps us determine the most appropriate methods and techniques for further analysis.
