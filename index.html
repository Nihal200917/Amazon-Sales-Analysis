Amazon Sales Profit Prediction

Introduction

Sales Profit by preferred Sales Channels have grown tremendously. I gathered information from Amazon Sales Kaggle dataset, covering 100 occurred sales throughout years from 2010 to 2019. we used this information to predict the result of sales profits in upcoming years depending on sales channels. We modeled Total Profit, a numerical variable, as a linear function of Order Years. As year increases Profit seams to increase. The Sales Profit in the Online Sales Channel is predicted to be increasing by $971 as each year advance, but this depends on external factors that might change the income profit for Amazon.
